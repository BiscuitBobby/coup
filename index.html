THE RULES

Objective

Be the last player with influence. Each player starts with 2 cards (kept secret) and a handful of coins.

Basic Actions (anyone can take these)
IncomeTake 1 coin. Cannot be blocked or challenged.
Foreign AidTake 2 coins. Can be blocked by the Duke.
CoupPay 7 coins — force any player to lose an influence. Unstoppable. Mandatory at 10+ coins.
Character Actions (claim the card — or bluff)
Duke
Tax: take 3 coins
Blocks Foreign Aid
Assassin
Pay 3 — force a loss of influence

Blocks stealing

Challenges & Blocks
ChallengeCall anyone's bluff. If they can't prove the card they claimed, they lose an influence — but if they can, you lose one instead, and they redraw.
BlockClaim a blocking character to stop an action. The actor can challenge your block — same stakes apply.
Losing Influence

When you lose influence you reveal one of your cards face-up permanently. Lose both and you're eliminated. 3 copies of each character exist in the deck.
